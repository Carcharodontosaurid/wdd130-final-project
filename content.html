Finding Your Style

Did you know there is more than one style of yoyo? Some are probably more familiar to you (such as responsive and looping), and others may be more suprising (like 3A or Freehand). But we're here to walk you through each one to find your perfect style.

1a (Looping)

This is probably what most people think of when they think of yoyo. This is the most common and most versitile style on this list. Most tricks involve loops and landing the yoyo back on sections of string and 'hopping' them between different configurations. Some of these are done to make pictures of the string, aptly named "picture tricks." Rock the Baby and Eiffiel tower are great examples of these.
