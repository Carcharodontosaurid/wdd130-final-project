Overview

Purpose

  1. I’m trying to get more people into yo-yoing with a website.
  2. This website is built to help show people what is possible to get them excited about it, and them show them how they can start learning.
  3. The site is partitioned out for different kinds of tricks and play styles so everyone can find something they like.
  4. Have you ever wanted to get into something or start a project, but you weren’t sure where to start or what you might need? This site takes all the guess work out of starting your yo-yo journey so you can jump right into it and find what you like, whether it’s simple picture tricks or off-string speed combos. Everything you would need to know to get started would be right at your fingertips.

Audience

This yoyo based website is meant to be a resource for both new players and seasoned competetors to find and learn new ways to play. For those unfamiliar with the craft, they can find what they need to get started, including gear, simple tricks, and an idea of what kind of style they hope to get into, as well as providing more advanced tricks and advice for those who know their way around a green triangle.

Typography

Heading Font: [Poiret One]

It's thin and flowy, like yoyo string. It's also a sans-serif font, so it can pair better with the paragraph font.

Paragraph Font: [Maitree]

It's attractive without being distracting and can be read easily on the page. It's also a serif font, so it paris well with the headings.
