Welcome To the Exciting World of Professional Yoyo!

Do you remember that time when you were a kid and some guy came to your elemntary school and did a bunch of wild yoyo tricks for an assembly, and it was pretty much the coolest thing you had ever seen (or at least seen at school)? We'd bet that almost every kid in your school had their own yoyo and was 'walking the dog' down the hall within the week. We'd also bet that almost every one of those yoyos disappeared a week later, and with them went everyone's dreams of being as good as Dan the Yoyo Man. Here at Infinite Triangle, our goal is to restore that same sense of wonder and excitement you had after that assembly, and give you the tools to succeed at becoming the best player you can be.

Finding Your Play Style

Just like a caricature artist or web designer, each yoyo player has their own specific flare. Whether you like dizzying speed combos, mind-bending slack tricks, or off-axis finger grinds, there's a yoyo for you!
